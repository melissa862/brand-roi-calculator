# Product Requirements Document

## Overview

An interactive calculator that shows B2B brands their **cost-per-outcome** and **ROI** for a LinkedIn creator partnership versus equivalent LinkedIn ad spend.

## Inputs

- Creator followers
- Engagement rate
- Content type
- Creator rate
- Industry
- Campaign goal
- Timeframe

## Outputs

- Side-by-side cost comparison
- ROI multiplier
- Hidden-cost breakdown of ads
- Qualitative benefits of creators

## End Goal

A shareable, embeddable lead-generation tool.
